Sage Hill Robotics gives students the opportunity to develop mechanical, electrical, and software engineering skills while designing, building, and testing competitive robots for FIRST Robotics Competition.

About Our Team

Formed at Sage Hill School in Newport Coast, California, our team participates in FIRSTÂ® Robotics Competition along with thousands of teams and students worldwide. We strive to provide real-world engineering experiences through competitive robotics while fostering a sense of community and teamwork.

Our team values innovation and uses cutting-edge technologies including 3D printing, welding, woodworking, and advanced manufacturing techniques. We're known for energizing school events with robot demonstrations and inspiring the next generation of engineers.

What We Do

Design & Build

We design and build competitive robots using CAD software, advanced manufacturing, and innovative engineering solutions.

Programming

Our software team develops autonomous routines and teleoperated control systems for our competition robots.

Competition

We compete in FIRST Robotics Competition regionals and work towards qualifying for the World Championship.

Outreach

We inspire younger students through demonstrations and mentoring, spreading STEM education in our community.
